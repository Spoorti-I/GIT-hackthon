🔒 Privacy

What Threat Breathing Style does and does not do with your data.

✅
Analysis runs locally

All heuristic checks run inside the extension. No URL is ever sent to our servers.

✅
No browsing history stored

Results are cached in memory only (per session). They are lost when the browser closes.

✅
No account required

PhishGuard works entirely without registration or login.

⚠️
Google Safe Browsing (optional)

If enabled, URLs are sent to Google's Safe Browsing API. Google's privacy policy applies. You can disable this at any time.

✅
Content script (optional)

Only counts password input fields. Never reads field values or user input.

ℹ️ About

PhishGuard

Version 1.0.0

Real-time phishing detection powered by multi-layer heuristic analysis. Designed for speed, privacy, and explainability.

Scoring Thresholds

Safe Score 0 – 19 No significant risk signals detected.
Suspicious Score 20 – 49 One or more risk factors present. Proceed with caution.
Phishing Score 50+ Strong phishing indicators. Do not enter credentials.

Performance

Heuristic analysis typically completes in <5ms. Threat intel checks are async and update the badge when complete.
